# 🇮🇳 SmartBhaarat – GenAI Powered Civic Platform

> **Empowering Citizens. Simplifying Governance. Enhancing Transparency with Generative AI.**

SmartBhaarat is an AI-powered civic platform developed during **PromptWars**, organized by **Devengers** in collaboration with **Google for Developers** and **Hack2Skill**.

The platform bridges the gap between citizens and government services through an intelligent AI companion that simplifies complex government information, recommends relevant public services, assists with document verification, enables smart issue reporting, and provides multilingual support for a seamless digital governance experience.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Citizens often face challenges while accessing government services due to:

- Complex government procedures
- Difficulty understanding eligibility criteria
- Lack of multilingual assistance
- Confusing documentation requirements
- Inefficient complaint reporting and tracking
- Limited accessibility and digital inclusion

SmartBhaarat addresses these challenges using **Generative AI** to create a more intuitive, transparent, and citizen-friendly governance platform.
